Green Bean Salad with Eggs

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken egg 4
Garlic 2
Green string beans 400g
Butter 1 tablespoon
Mayonnaise 1

Step 1: Boil hard-boiled eggs and cover with cold water..

Step 2: Peel and chop the green beans. Boil water, salt to taste, add beans and boil until desired softness (5â€“10 minutes). Rinse the prepared beans in a colander under running cold water.

Step 3: Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a frying pan over medium heat, add green beans and fry with oil for 5 minutes, stirring

Step 4: Put the fried beans in a salad bowl, squeeze garlic on top.

Step 5: Peel the eggs and slice them into a salad bowl with beans.

Step 6: Salt to taste, season with mayonnaise and mix.

French Fried Eggs

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken egg 5
Sour cream 1 bank
Spices 1
Salt 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Pour the sour cream on a hot skillet without oil.

Step 2: Wait until the liquid from sour cream is evaporated - then gently pour the eggs, salt and pepper to taste and cover the pan.

Step 3: Immediately after that, turn off the gas and allow the eggs to cool.

Potato soup

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 7
Champignon 300 g
Onion 1
Cream 200 ml
Butter 20 g
Salt 1
Greens 10 g

Step 1: Peel potatoes, cut into cubes, pour water so that the potatoes were completely covered. Water salt.

Step 2: In a frying pan, drown the butter and fry the finely chopped onion until golden brown. Then cut into large pieces of mushrooms and add to the onions. Slightly fry (if it starts to burn, add a little water from the potatoes).

Step 3: Fried onions with mushrooms are sent to the pan with the prepared potatoes and cook a little more, stirring occasionally.

Step 4: Merge the broth into a separate bowl. A potato and mushroom mass, beat with a blender. After, add to the resulting puree broth until it becomes thinner, until you need consistency.

Step 5: We put on the fire, heat constantly stirring. Introduce the cream and mix everything well. Bring to a boil and remove from heat.

Step 6: Serve, decorated with greens and a couple of slices of mushrooms.
